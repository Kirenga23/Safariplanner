💡 Money-saving Tips · Vidokezo vya Kuokoa Pesa
🗓️Travel Apr–Jun (shoulder) — 20–30% cheaper than peak season
🚌Road transfers between Serengeti & Ngorongoro save ~$400 vs flying
🦍Book Bwindi gorilla permits direct at ugandawildlife.org — no agent markup
🏕️Public campsites in Tanzanian parks cost $30–50/night vs $300+ for lodges
👥Groups of 4+ split vehicle hire — biggest single saving on a safari
